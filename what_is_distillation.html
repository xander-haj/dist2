What is Model Distillation?

Model distillation is the process of compressing the complex knowledge of a massive "Teacher" model into a compact "Student" model, preserving accuracy while drastically reducing computational cost.

The Core Intuition

Imagine a university professor (the Teacher) who has spent 40 years studying physics...

In Deep Learning terms:

  • The Teacher: A massive model...
  • The Student: A smaller architecture...
  • The Knowledge: Not just the final answer...

Why Distill?

Latency

A 70B model might generate 5 tokens/second...

Cost

Deploying a 70B model requires ~140GB of VRAM...
