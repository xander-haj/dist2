Distillation vs. Other Compression Techniques

Distillation is not the only way to make models smaller. It often works best when combined with Quantization and Pruning.

Comparison Table

Technique Mechanism Pros Cons
Distillation Training a smaller architecture... Changes architecture structure... Expensive to train...
Quantization Reducing precision... Instant memory reduction... Loss of precision...
Pruning Removing neurons... Can create sparse models... Often results in sparse matrices...
LoRA Freezing weights... Very cheap training memory... Does not reduce inference size...

The "Distill-then-Quantize" Pipeline

The industry standard for local deployment is to combine these:

  1. Distill: Train a 7B student...
  2. Quantize: Convert the 7B student...
